Gathering Investors Information

Create a persona of Indian investors who typically invest at the pre-seed and seed stage. They should focus on SaaS startups, consumer-centric businesses (including health and wellness), and startups that use light AI. Prioritize investors who are based in India, especially in Bengaluru, Mumbai, Delhi NCR, and Hyderabad. Include roles such as Managing Partner, General Partner, Founding Partner, Principal, Angel Investor, and Venture Partner. Target firms that are seed funds, micro VCs, or early-stage venture capital firms.
